Cleveland Food Bank

The primary food bank for supporting those in need around Cleveland. Not just the city proper -- all around the metro area.

An interesting note, if its meaningful at all to you: there's actually a good amount of people up from the South/Central Americas that have ended up in Painesville/Mentor, to do with a housing relocation program they ran a while ago. And just a lot of elderly, people out of work. Its tough times out there.
